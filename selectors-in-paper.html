Paper Kit / Patterns / Context selectors · in-paper

Two pickers,
inside the paper.

A quieter pattern. No sticky bar above the page — the selectors sit in the top-right of the report itself, paired with the title block on the left. Institution and peer group only; time range is left to a separate moment.

00 · the bar — closed

The default.

Two triggers, equal-weight, no chrome. Each carries a mono label and a display-sans value. The teal highlighter on the active value signals where attention should go.

The bar is sticky and always visible. A change in any selector reloads the page beneath, with a soft 320ms settle.
